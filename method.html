6D Method

Over the years I have developed a simple yet sophisticated method for solving complex & multifaceted marketing problems creatively. This helps us pre-define the desired emotional response from various user groups so we can channel all the marketing efforts towards achieving that response.

1. DISCOVERY

helping uncover valuable information - i.e. questionnaire, interview, analysis

2. DEFINITION

helping compile that information and articulate what the brand stands for - i.e. word cloud, mood board, emotions etc.

3. DESTINCTION

helping condense the definition in a unique set of words - campaign name, values, skills and SWOT analysis

4. DESIRABILITY

making the above defined uniqueness valuable for others - e.g. taglines, catch lines and headlines, vision/mission etc.

5. DESIGN

sculpting the brand's existence - i.e. logos, colors, mockups etc.

6. DELIVERY

bringing the brand to life - i.e. activation and brand book: environment, brand assets, signage, stationery etc.
