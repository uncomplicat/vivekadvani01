The power of belonging

In ancient times, business leaders believed that the customer was king. As some years passed, managers started realising that if they treated the employees right, they will take care of their customers. So the employees became the kings. But today as we understand human brains and our emotions a little bit better, we are learning the importance of having an inclusive community. At their core, people want to feel included & the future of businesses is community-first. When people feel like they belong to a group and are contributing towards a bigger cause, they automatically give their best selves - be it customers, employees or shareholders.

Just don't forget to add the most important ingredient called: personal freedom.
